ABOUT US

We are pleased to welcome you to store of jewelry findings by Anna Chernykh! Here you can buy findings to making jewelry. Many elements can be used in other kinds of art - clothes modeling, puppet making, leather working, and more. All findings are designed and manufactured in Russia.

If you're new here, start acquaintance with our products from the page "Catalog". Our findings produced by collections, formed in style. It is also possible to search for items on the functional purpose - bead caps, spacers, toggle clasps.

If you don`t understand any term, check out the "Information" section, where you can read the master-classes for working with our findings. On the page "Gallery" you can find the works of artists who use our findings.

If you did not find the necessary information, please contact us - details are given on the "Contact" page.

We hope you enjoy our findings and you will become our regular guest :)

Anna Chernykh
