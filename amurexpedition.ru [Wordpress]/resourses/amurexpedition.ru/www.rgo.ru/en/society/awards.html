Awards

nbkjhgl.jpg


The Russian Geographical Society has a right to adjudge awards for outstanding achievements and accomplishments in the field of geography and related sciences of nature and society, as well as organizational and other assistance to the Society. The Society has a right to nominate and support candidates for being awarded honorary titles of the Society, awards and prizes, awarded by the Russian Federation, Russian Academy of Sciences and other organizations and institutions, as well as governments and organizations of foreign states.
